    About

    The main motivation of this project is to enable a reliable way to prepare for the Service Selection Boards Exam.

    The idea for creating this began when Sagar Tamang's big brother Paraj Bahadur Tamang (as seen in the image), who is currently in Officer's Training Academy Chennai (OTA Chennai), suggested a need for an app that could provide a seamless way to practice for Service Selection Board's Tests.

    Soon enough, Sagar Tamang began building the entire project under his guidance.
